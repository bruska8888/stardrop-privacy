StarDrop — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

StarDrop ("the App") is a casual arcade game for iOS. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

2. Data we collect

The App does not collect any personal data. It does not require account registration, does not ask for your name, email address, location, contacts, or any other personal information.

All game progress (star balance, high score) is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

3. In-App Purchases

The App offers consumable In-App Purchases ("Stars") processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not handle or store any payment information. Apple's Privacy Policy governs purchase data: apple.com/legal/privacy.

4. Analytics and advertising

The App does not use any third-party analytics SDKs (e.g. Firebase, Amplitude, Mixpanel) and does not display advertising of any kind.

5. Third-party services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, social networks, or external APIs beyond Apple's own StoreKit for In-App Purchases.

6. Children's privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Because the App collects no personal data at all, no special handling for children is required.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the App after any change constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
